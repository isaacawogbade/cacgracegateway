Join the Team

Volunteering is a great way to help make a positive impact in the world, and to build friendships. You are a unique individual that can contribute in a way nobody else can. When we all work together, we add immeasurable beauty to the universe and fulfill God's purpose.

Volunteer With Us Through the Following Ministries

Ushering & Security

The Ushering and Security Ministry aims at maintaining order whenever the church services or any other meetings are on. It has the responsibility of tidying up the church facilities before, during, and after the church sessions; including, though not limited to, the church halls, compound, seats, podium, carpets, draperies, potted plants etc. Through this ministry worshipers and visitors are ushered into church and to the seats. Ushers also help in ensuring there is order during altar calls and assist in receiving offerings and tithes. This ministry is also incharge of protocol and hospitality services.

Music Team

The Praise and Worship Ministry leads the congregation in praise and worship songs. Through it an atmosphere of the Lord’s presence is established in every service. It is a platform for raising and nurturing new members who are gifted in singing and leading worship. Through this ministry members who are gifted in the playing of various kinds of instruments are raised. Through it, members learn new songs. The ministry produces Spirit-led original songs for corporate worship in church. The congregation is also taught the biblical worldview of praise and worship.

Women's Fellowship

The Women's Fellowship has the principal objective of bringing all women together for growth in spiritual, family, business, and social life. The department also raises God fearing young unmarried ladies to be adequately prepared for family life. It also raises God fearing wives who are submissive to their husbands and are well equipped to raise and nurture children. The fellowship brings women together for prayer and fosters unity among women.

Men's Fellowship

The Men’s Fellowship aims at bringing all men together in order to equip them for spiritual, family business and general social life. It also aims at nurturing unmarried young men to be God fearing and adequately prepared to take up leadership in their families once married. Men are taught to be responsible gentlemen who love their wives and model Christ in raising their children. The Men’s fellowship is keen on bringing men together for prayer, foster unity among themselves, and influence un-churched men within the community through such activities like breakfast meetings, business seminars.

Kids Ministry

The Kid’s Ministry aims at nurturing and training children in the ways of God with the aim of finally producing strong God Fearing Youth. We walk with the children the journey of knowing Christ across the various stages of their early life, from infancy through teenage. Children get to know the word of God and are taught how to pray by themselves. They are nurtured to obey their parents and all other authorities God has placed over them. Their talents are gifts and are nurtured personally. We also ensure that the Kid’s Ministry integrates appropriately with the rest of the body of Christ.

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry aims at bringing the youth together for fellowship and equips them spiritually and socially. The ministry nurtures and grows youth talents, skills and gifts. They are developed into a God fearing generation equipped for excellence in the marketplace. The ministry aims at raising a new crop of leaders who are ready to take up positions of responsibility within the ministry and in the community. The youth are prepared for the subsequent stages of life: those who have graduated from schools for life after school, and those graduating from colleges and universities for the life thereafter. They are also coached on relationships with the opposite sex besides being prepared for married life.

Evangelism and Missions Team

The main objective of the Evangelism and Missions Team is to preach the Gospel to the unreached. These are reached in their homes, businesses, neighborhood, shopping centers, markets, towns, cities, villages, and in hard- to- reach remote places. The evangelistic campaigns are also taken to schools, college, hospitals, prisons, and foreign nations. The following approaches are employed: one -to -one evangelism, open-air crusades, use of handbills, drama and movies, TV and radio, Internet, and social media.

Technical and Media Team

The Technical and Media Team exists to invest ourselves in a ministry that will directly support and reinforce the presentation of Biblical truths that requires the support of lighting, audio, video, sound or any other form of technical support during church services, events as well as stage support. We strive to do this in a manner that is honouring to our God. The Technical Department will strive for technical excellence. We will endeavour to understand not only the technical aspects of the equipment but also the environment in which it is used. We will do so in a Christ-like manner.
