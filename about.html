About Us

In 1920 Christ Apostolic Church, Grace Gateway Grays (Solution Center District) was established by the then District Pastor, Dr. James Olajuyigbe etc.. We are committed to building a Pentecostal Church of dedicated and matured believers who are dynamic, walking in the fruit of the Spirit and demonstrating the gifts of the Holy Spirit through the application of God’s word.

Our Vision Statement

"Empowering Souls and Imparting Lives." - Luke 4:18-19

Our Mission Statement

Word

To build Pentecostal Church of dedicated and matured believers who are dynamic, waling in the fruit of the Spirit and demonstrating the gift of the Holy Spirit through the application of God's word.

Worship

To raise up winners, full of joy, holiness and assurance of salvation, irrespective of their age, gender, colour, education, cultural or socio-economic status.

Welfare

To put into operation practical, purposeful, spiritual and community oriented programmes/ministries.

Witness

To penetrate and saturate Great Britain, Europe and the rest of the Worldwith the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Warfare

To raise people who love prayer and are committed to praying both privately and publicly and to all make Heaven together.
